Chicken Curry

Decription

Chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish, popular in various cuisines around the world. It typically consists of tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, spicy sauce made with a blend of herbs, spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk or yogurt, offering a perfect balance of heat and depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (breast or thigh), cut into bite-sized chunks
  • Cooking oil
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Curry powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground turmeric
  • Canned tomatoes, crushed or diced
  • Coconut milk or yogurt
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, to serve

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. Brown the Chicken: In a large skillet or pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until it is lightly browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the Aromatics: In the same pan, add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent.
  4. Add the Spices: Stir in the curry powder, ground cumin, and ground turmeric, cooking for about a minute until fragrant.
  5. Combine with Tomatoes: Add the canned tomatoes to the pan and stir well, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  6. Simmer the Curry: Return the chicken to the pan. Add the coconut milk or yogurt, and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat and cook, covered, for about 20-30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Check for Seasoning: Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or spices as needed.
  8. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve the chicken curry with cooked rice or naan bread.
